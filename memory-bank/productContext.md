# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Hal51 AI needs a platform to articulate its dual focus on Education and AI Safety. The company started with educational hardware (LemurBox) but identified data and AI safety as critical "moats" and necessities for safe educational AI tools.

## Solution
A clean, professional website that tells this story:
1.  **Origin**: Education & LemurBox (DIY holograms).
2.  **Evolution**: Realization of AI Safety importance.
3.  **Current Mission**: Building safe, aligned AI for education to benefit mankind.

## User Experience Goals
- **Clarity**: Visitors should immediately understand what Hal51 AI does.
- **Credibility**: Highlighting the strong academic and industry background of the founders (Vinay, Sang, Vikranth).
- **Accessibility**: Easy access to their research (arXiv papers).
